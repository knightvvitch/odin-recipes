Pumpkin Bread

Dense and Delicious!

If you like your bread sweet, spiced, and denser than a neutron star, then you are in for one heck of a treat. This Pumpkin Bread is like an entire fall festival in your mouth!

Here is where I tell you the story of growing up on my great-grandmother's farm and how she used to grow pumpkins so tender that you would think she was made of pumpkin herself. Us kids would gather 'round the hearth to munch on freshly roasted pumpkin seeds and spit the shells right into the fire! Ah, memories. Absolutely hazardous heartwarming. You don't care about my life story and are only here so that you can make the best pumpkin bread this side of the county line? Sigh, fine. Here you go.

Ingredients

  • 1 12oz can of pumpkin
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 12 tbsp softened butter
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp cloves
  • 1 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Cream the butter and sugar together
  3. Add the eggs, vanilla, and pumpkin
  4. In a separate bowl, mix the flour, baking soda, baking powder, spices, and salt together
  5. Fold the dry ingredients into the wet
  6. Grease 2 9" loaf pans
  7. Split the mixture into the 2 pans
  8. Bake for 45 mins or until a toothpick comes out of the middle clean
  9. Cool for 30 minutes, slice, and enjoy!
